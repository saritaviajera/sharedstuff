HAMBURG
also know as the gateway to the world due to famous Hamburg Port is my hometown and one of the most beautiful cities in the world.

What's so good about this city, that even The Beatles started their career here?
Let me tell you:
There are more than 2.500 bridges in Hamburg. Tiny bridges, huge bridges, for pedestrians and cars... that's even more than in Venice!
Did you know you can visit the worlds largest miniature train exhibition, called Minaturwunderland in Hamburg?
We have an old warehouse turned into an opera house. How wild is that?
Our Elphi (Elbphilharmonie) is one of the newest additions to Hamburg's amazing harbour panorama! See the beauty herself in the picture above.

Last but not least, in Hamburg we say Moin instead of Hello.
